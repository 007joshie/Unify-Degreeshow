biography

Hello, I’m Eden Halley. I’m a passionate graphic designer soon to graduate from Waikato University with a degree in communication design and marketing. I’ve always had a flare in the arts and Waikato University has guided me to a degree that helps me to expand on those creative processes.

With a drive to think outside of the box, I always knew that Communication Design and Marketing would be the perfect combination of degrees to pursue. Surprisingly Waikato was the only school that allowed me to achieve this at the time and completing my degree at Waikato has supported me in finding the perfect combination of my passions and understanding how design and marketing work together harmoniously to create the perfect blend of advertising.
