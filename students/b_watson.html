biography

Hello, I’m Briana Watson and I will soon be graduating with a Bachelor of Design majoring in Media and minoring in Communication Design.

To me, design means creating your point of difference in a competitive industry, making your mark and creating your own style. This degree has helped me learn the fundamentals of design and how to apply them in the industry. I’ve learnt many skills using adobe products that now allow me to produce my own content at a high level. From marketing, graphics, photos and videos, I now have the tools and skill set to excel at what I do and turn my vision into reality.

project description

My project is to promote the Flex Fitness Waikato gyms to people living in the Waikato. The message of this video is to show what the facilities have to offer and the importance of health and well-being.
