biography

First Paragraph: Hi guys, I’m Amon Mohamed and I will soon be graduating with a Bachelor of Communication Design.

Design to me is a space where people bring their ideas & creativity to life. Waikato has made me a better designer for sure because when I first started design all I knew how to use was photoshop, but after 3 years I have learnt many techniques, such as the design principles and have learnt how to use different programs like illustrator, adobe after effects, and adobe InDesign, which helped me become a better a designer. Throughout the years of doing design, I have found that I’m more suited with app design but I do like making posters and designing websites.

project description

My project’s main purpose is to teach folding techniques to semi organised males between the ages of 18 to 25, who like fashion and bright colours. I feel like this is a useful skill to learn because It has a lot of benefits like saving room in your wardrobe or suitcase.
